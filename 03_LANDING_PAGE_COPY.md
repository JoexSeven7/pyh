## VIII. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ SECTION)

### Category 1: Hours & Availability

**Q: Are you really open 24 hours?**
A: "Yes! Medplus Pharmacy is open 24 hours, 7 days a week, including weekends and public holidays. We're here for emergencies anytime."

**Q: What are your peak hours?**
A: "We're busiest between 8 AM - 6 PM on weekdays. However, we always have staff available and can serve you at any time of day or night."

**Q: Do you have extended delivery hours?**
A: "Yes! We offer delivery until 10 PM daily, with special arrangements available for urgent cases. Contact us for late-night delivery options."

---

### Category 2: Ordering & Delivery

**Q: How do I place an order?**
A: "You can order three ways: (1) Call us directly at 0905 549 2610, (2) WhatsApp your order to our number, or (3) Visit us in person at 32 Emmanuel Keshi Street. We'll confirm delivery time with you."

**Q: Do you offer same-day delivery?**
A: "Yes! Orders placed before 2 PM are typically delivered same-day within Lagos Island and Mainland areas near our Magodo location. Delivery time depends on location."

**Q: What's your delivery fee?**
A: "Delivery fees start from ₦500 for orders below ₦5,000, and delivery is FREE on orders over ₦10,000. Exact fee depends on your location. We'll confirm before you confirm your order."

**Q: What's your service area for delivery?**
A: "We primarily serve Magodo, Ketu, Ikorodu Road, and surrounding areas. For deliveries outside our main area, call us first to confirm."

**Q: Can I track my delivery?**
A: "Yes! Once we dispatch your order, we'll send you tracking details via WhatsApp or phone. You can contact us anytime for updates."

**Q: What if I'm not home for my delivery?**
A: "We'll try multiple times on the delivery day. If you're unavailable, we can arrange a different delivery time. Call us to reschedule."

### Category 3: Prescriptions & Medications

**Q: Do I need a prescription?**
A: "For prescription medications, yes—you'll need a valid prescription from a licensed doctor. For over-the-counter items, no prescription needed. We also offer pharmacist consultations."

**Q: Can I transfer my prescription to Medplus?**
A: "Yes! You can bring your original prescription, or for existing prescriptions, your previous pharmacy can transfer them to us. We can handle this quickly."

**Q: What if my medication isn't in stock?**
A: "We carry a wide range of medications. If we don't have it, our team can often source it within 24-48 hours. We'll confirm availability and price with you first."

**Q: Do you offer generic alternatives?**
A: "Yes! We can suggest quality generic alternatives if available. Our pharmacists will explain the differences and help you choose what's best for your needs and budget."

**Q: Can I get medication refills?**
A: "Absolutely! You can call ahead or visit us anytime. Most refills are processed immediately. For some controlled substances, you'll need an updated prescription."

**Q: Do you have a pharmacist available for consultation?**
A: "Yes! Our licensed pharmacists are available 24/7 to answer questions, check for drug interactions, and provide guidance. Consultations are FREE."

A: "No minimum order required! We deliver orders of any size. However, we do charge delivery fees that may make very small orders less economical."

## XV. TONE & VOICE GUIDELINES FOR WRITERS

✅ **DO:**

- Be warm, professional, and approachable
- Use "we/us" (personal, community-focused)
- Be clear and direct
- Address customer pain points
- Emphasize 24/7 availability
- Show expertise without being condescending
- Use inclusive language

❌ **DON'T:**

- Be overly clinical or sterile
- Use medical jargon without explanation
- Make claims you can't back up
- Be pushy or salesy
- Ignore the "slow response" pain point
- Use outdated language
- Assume customers are not health-literate
