## FAQ PAGE DESIGN RECOMMENDATIONS

### Layout Options:

1. **Accordion Style** (Most Common)

   - Questions collapsed by default
   - Click to expand answer
   - One question open at a time
   - Smooth animation

2. **Category Tabs**

   - Six category tabs at top
   - Click tab to show only those FAQs
   - Good for organization

3. **Hybrid Approach** (Recommended)
   - Category tabs + accordion items
   - Best UX for large FAQ sections

### Design Features:

- **Search Bar** at the top for users to search keywords
- **Quick Links** to jump to category
- **Print-Friendly Version** option
- **Related Questions** suggestions
- **"Still have questions?"** CTA linking to contact form

### Mobile Optimization:

- Stack tabs vertically on mobile
- Ensure accordion is touch-friendly
- Large tap targets (48px minimum)
- Collapse answers by default to reduce scrolling

### Accessibility:

- Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- ARIA labels for accordion buttons
- Keyboard navigation (Tab, Enter to expand)
- High color contrast

### SEO Optimization:

- Use proper schema markup (FAQSchema)
- Include keywords naturally in Q&A
- One H1 per page ("Frequently Asked Questions")
- Meta description: "Get answers to common questions about Medplus Pharmacy‚Äî24-hour pharmacy in Magodo with delivery, prescriptions, and beauty products."
